Spot the bias

Can you catch bias as it scrolls by?

A feed moves past you. Some posts carry quiet stereotypes; some are fair. Flag the biased ones before they slip away. Trust your gut, then see what you caught.

How much do you actually know?

No grades, no pressure: a few questions to test what's stuck and fill in what hasn't. Every answer comes with the why.

Myth or real?

Bust the myths.

Swipe each card: left if it's a myth, right if it's real. Trust your gut, then learn the truth behind it.
