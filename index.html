Why this exists

Students told us the same two things, again and again: they want somewhere to speak, and someone to answer.

Many said they’d only share if they could stay anonymous — so here, anonymity isn’t a setting. It’s the default.

And Black girls told us their experiences were treated as an afterthought. On this site, Black feminism isn’t a chapter — it’s the foundation.
